I am the creator of the website “Comportfolio”. As a college student currently exploring the field of computer science, I have always been fascinated by the connection between technology and creativity. This website is a reflection of my journey through the Introduction to Computing course, where I gained and honed valuable skills in web designing, programming, and project management.
